ABOUT US

Our products are freshly homemade, with no added preservatives or chemicals. Each batch is crafted with love for your family’s enjoyment, perfect for any occasion.

We began as a kitchen hobby blossomed into a one-woman business in San Jose del Monte, Bulacan. We wanted to share homemade goodness with everyone, always made with care and quality ingredients. We prioritize fresh, preservative-free products, supporting local suppliers, and giving every order a personal touch.

Orders are collected before production to ensure freshness, and shipping outside Manila may take a few days.

Thank you for supporting small businesses and helping us share homemade goodness!
